  # Privacy Policy
                          
    Effective Date: May 3, 2026                                                                                
  Last Updated: May 3, 2026                                                                                  
                                                                                                             
  1. Introduction                                                                                            
                                                
  Meet ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Meet mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains 
  how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App. Please read this policy
   carefully. By using the App, you consent to the practices described here.                                 
                                                
  If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the App.                                           
   
  ---                                                                                                        
                                                
  2. Information We Collect

  2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  - Account information: Email address and password when you register                                        
  - Profile information: Name, date of birth, gender, profile photos, biography, and interests
  - Preferences: Gender preference and connection intent (dating, friendship, networking)                    
  - Communications: Messages sent to other users through the App's chat feature                              
  - Reports: Content you submit when reporting another user                                                  
                                                                                                             
  2.2 Information Collected Automatically                                                                    
                                                                                                             
  - Precise location data: When you use the App, we collect your real-time GPS location to show you people   
  nearby and to show others your approximate location. Location is collected while the App is in the
  foreground.                                                                                                
  - Last seen timestamp: The time you were last active in the App
  - Device information: Operating system, device type, and unique device identifiers for push notification   
  delivery                                                                                                   
                                                                                                             
  2.3 Information from Third Parties                                                                         
                                                
  We use Supabase as our backend infrastructure. Authentication, database storage, and file storage are      
  processed through Supabase's platform. Please review Supabase's privacy policy at supabase.com/privacy.
                                                                                                             
  ---                                           
                                               
  3. How We Use Your Information

  We use the information we collect to:                                                                      
   
  - Provide core features: Show you nearby users, process waves and matches, enable chat                     
  - Display your profile: Show your photos, bio, interests, and other profile details to other users within
  your configured radius                                                                                     
  - Share your location: Broadcast your approximate location to other active users within the App so they can
   see you in their nearby feed                                                                              
  - Send notifications: Deliver push notifications for new waves, matches, and messages
  - Safety and moderation: Process reports of inappropriate behavior, enforce blocks, and take action on     
  violations of our Terms of Service                                                                         
  - Improve the App: Analyze usage patterns to fix bugs and improve features                                 
  - Legal compliance: Comply with applicable laws and respond to lawful requests                             
                                                                                                             
  ---                                          
                                                                                                             
  4. How We Share Your Information              
                                               
  4.1 With Other Users                                                                                       
   
  When you use Meet, the following profile information is visible to other users within your location radius:
  - Name, age (derived from birthday), gender   
  - Profile photos                                                                                           
  - Bio and interests                           
  - Connection intent                                                                                        
  - Approximate location (shown as distance in feet)
                                                                                                             
  Your exact coordinates are never displayed to other users — only a calculated distance.                    
                                                                                                             
  When you send a wave, the recipient sees your name, photo, and the neighborhood/area name from which you   
  waved.                                        
                                                                                                             
  4.2 With Service Providers                    
                                               
  We share data with third-party vendors who help us operate the App:                                        
  - Supabase: Database, authentication, and file storage
  - Apple (APNs): Push notification delivery                                                                 
                                                
  These providers are contractually obligated to use your data only to perform services for us.              
   
  4.3 For Legal Reasons                                                                                      
                                                
  We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government request, or if we believe  
  disclosure is necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of Meet, our users, or the public.
                                                                                                             
  4.4 Business Transfers                                                                                     
                                               
  If Meet is acquired, merged, or its assets are sold, your information may be transferred as part of that   
  transaction. We will notify you via email or a prominent in-app notice before your data becomes subject to
  a different privacy policy.                                                                                
                                                
  4.5 No Sale of Personal Data                                                                               
   
  We do not sell your personal information to third parties.                                                 
                                                
  ---                                          

  5. Location Data

  Location is central to Meet's core functionality. Specifically:                                            
   
  - Real-time location is collected while you actively use the App and is used to populate the nearby feed   
  for other users                               
  - Your location is broadcast to other active users in the App who are within range — this is an            
  intentional, core feature of the service                                                                   
  - Location is updated approximately every 30 seconds while the App is open
  - You can disable location access at any time through your device settings, but doing so will prevent the  
  App from functioning                                                                                       
  - We do not collect location data when the App is in the background or closed                              
                                                                                                             
  ---                                           
                                                                                                             
  6. Photos and Media                           
                                               
  Profile photos you upload are:                                                                             
  - Stored in our cloud storage (Supabase Storage)
  - Accessible to authenticated users of the App via short-lived signed URLs (24-hour expiration). They are not publicly indexable on the web.                                                          
  - Retained until you delete them or delete your account                                                    
                                                                                                             
  We do not use your photos to train machine learning models.                                                
                                                                                                             
  ---                                                                                                        
                                                
  7. Data Retention                            

  - Active accounts: We retain your data for as long as your account is active                               
  - Deleted accounts: When you delete your account, we delete your profile, photos, messages, matches, and
  waves within 30 days. Some data may be retained in backups for up to 90 days before being overwritten.     
  - Reports: Reports you submit or that are submitted about you may be retained for safety and legal
  compliance purposes even after account deletion                                                            
                                                
  ---                                                                                                        
                                                
  8. Your Rights and Choices                   

  Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  - Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you                                            
  - Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  - Deletion: Delete your account and associated data at any time via the "Delete Account" option in the     
  App's profile screen. This permanently deletes your profile, photos, matches, messages, and waves.         
  - Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  - Opt out of notifications: Disable push notifications via your device settings at any time                
                                                
  To exercise any right not available in-app, contact us at meet.app.nyc@gmail.com.                          
                                                
  California Residents (CCPA)                                                                                
                                                
  California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act, including the right 
  to know what personal information is collected, the right to delete, and the right to opt out of sale (we
  do not sell data). To submit a request, contact meet.app.nyc@gmail.com.                                    
                                                
  EEA/UK Residents (GDPR)                      

  If you are in the European Economic Area or United Kingdom, our lawful basis for processing is:            
  - Performance of a contract for core App functionality
  - Legitimate interests for safety, fraud prevention, and improvement of the App                            
  - Consent for push notifications and location access                                                       
                                               
  You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.                             
                                                
  ---                                          

  9. Children's Privacy

  Meet is intended exclusively for users 18 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect personal     
  information from anyone under 18. If we discover that a user is under 18, we will terminate their account
  and delete their data immediately. If you believe a minor has created an account, please contact us at     
  meet.app.nyc@gmail.com.

  10. Security

  We implement industry-standard security measures including:
  - Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  - Row-level security on all database tables (users can only access their own data and data explicitly      
  shared with them)
  - Authentication managed through Supabase Auth with secure session tokens                                  
                                                                                                             
  No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.
